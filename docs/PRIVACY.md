# Cadence — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-13_

Cadence is a local-first app. Your data stays on your device.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Cadence does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal
data. There is no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no account
server.

## Where your data lives

Everything you create in Cadence — tasks, goals, streaks, notes, photos you
attach, profile names and pictures, and app settings — is stored **only on your
device**, inside the app's local database (IndexedDB) and local preferences. It
never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

- **Email at sign-in:** the email you enter is a local-only label so the app can
  greet you. It is stored on-device and is **never sent anywhere** — there is no
  password and no network request.
- **Camera / photo library:** used only when you choose to attach a picture to a
  note. Photos you attach are stored locally with that note.
- **Exports:** when you tap "Export data" (PDF or JSON), the file is saved to
  your device or your chosen location. You control where it goes.

## Third parties

Cadence does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. The only
outbound link is the optional "Buy me a coffee" button, which opens Ko-fi in
your browser — visiting that page is governed by Ko-fi's own privacy policy.

## Data deletion

Because all data is local, you can erase everything at any time from
**You → Data → Reset all data**, or by deleting the app.

## Children

Cadence does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.
