# Cadence — Support

Need help with Cadence? You're in the right place.

## Common questions

**Where is my data?**
On your device only. Cadence is local-first — nothing is uploaded. See the
[Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).

**How do I back up or move my data?**
Go to **You → Data → Export data** and choose **JSON** for a full backup (or
**PDF** for a readable snapshot). Keep the JSON file somewhere safe.

**I signed in with an email — is that an account?**
No. The email is just a local label. There's no password and no server; it
never leaves your device.

**How do streaks work?**
Complete at least one task in a day, then tap **Check in today**. Missing a
task's deadline breaks the streak (shown in red on the heatmap).

**How do I attach a photo to a note?**
Open a note and tap **Photo**. Cadence asks for camera/photo-library access only
at that moment.

**How do I reset everything?**
**You → Data → Reset all data.** This erases all tasks, goals, notes, and
profiles on the device and cannot be undone.
